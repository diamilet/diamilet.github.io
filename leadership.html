2018: MIT Better World in Miami, Florida

Photo credit: Maria Paula Angarita


Click here to watch the video on Youtube.


I was invited to speak at the Miami event of the MIT Campaign for a Better World, a fundraising initiative aimed at advancing the institute's research and scholarship on some of the world's biggest challenges. In front of a crowd of around 300 people, I shared what inspired me to pursue a career in hurricane research, the societal impacts of this work, and how MIT has helped advance me towards that goal.

2017: Discover EAPS Extreme Weather and Climate

Photo credit: MIT EAPS

I served as a graduate teaching assistant for my department's freshman pre-orientation program, a 5-day exploration of the science of the Earth at MIT and at Mt. Washington in New Hampshire. We organized activities to engage incoming MIT students into learning about extreme weather, hurricanes and blizzards, and current topics of climate research.
